# Field

> Light with earth tones. Agricultural.

## When to use

Matches brand vibes: **Farm-focused/Down-to-earth**. Suitable for family farm dealers, down-to-earth vibes, dealers who specifically don't want a "tech company" feel.

No existing site uses this archetype yet.

## Design specification

- Theme: warm light palette — cream/amber/stone tones instead of cool grays. Dark accents in warm charcoal or forest green, not pure black.
- Hero: full-width agricultural photo background (field, harvest, aerial farm shot) instead of video. Warm gradient overlay. Centered or left-aligned text.
- Texture: subtle grain/noise texture on surfaces, organic shapes (rounded blobs, soft gradients), no geometric precision — feels natural and approachable.
- Cards: warm-toned surfaces (stone-100, amber-50) with soft borders, rounded corners, warm shadow tints. Hover lifts slightly.
- Product layout: large product image with warm-toned overlay, specs presented as a clean list or horizontal strip below. Less aggressive than Forge/Tactical — the product is important but the feel is approachable, not militaristic.
- Typography: mixed case (not all-caps headlines). Slightly softer weight (font-bold, not font-black). A display serif font for headlines paired with clean sans body would fit here — this is the one archetype where a Google Font could work well.
