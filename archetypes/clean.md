# Clean

> Light dominant. Corporate-professional.

## When to use

Matches brand vibes: **Clean/Professional**, **Luxury/Premium**. Good fit for dealers who want a B2B integrator feel — white/gray body sections, dark accent bars, card-based layouts, minimal texture.

Currently used by: **Great River** (accent `#00392b`, card-based, minimal texture, B2B integrator feel).

## Design specification

- Theme: white and gray-50 body sections, dark green/navy/charcoal for header, footer, and contact section. Minimal texture.
- Hero: full-viewport video with heavy brand-color gradient overlay (not just dark — tinted), left-aligned text, pill-badge eyebrow ("Authorized Terraplex Dealer" in a rounded border pill), feature chips below CTAs. Use the shared `heroVideo` with `heroPoster` as fallback.
- Texture: minimal to none. Clean borders, consistent border-radius (rounded-xl), subtle shadows. Depth through card layering, not atmospheric effects.
- Cards: white or gray-50 bg with gray-200 borders, rounded-xl, consistent shadow-sm. Product cards as unified equal-height columns with image header → stat strip → bullet list → CTA (card-component pattern).
- Product layout: side-by-side product cards (R-32 and I-19 as equal columns), each with image, 4-column stat strip, feature checklist, and CTA. Featured product gets accent-color border + badge bar.
- Typography: font-bold (not black) headlines, structured/informational tone. Inline label badges ("PRIMARY PLATFORMS") in bg-accent/10 rounded pills.
