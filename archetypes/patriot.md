# Patriot

> Bold, American-flag-inspired.

## When to use

Matches brand vibes: **Patriotic**. Suitable for **Truss Services** (their questionnaire specifically selected "Patriotic" vibe with red/white/blue colors).

No existing site uses this archetype yet.

## Design specification

- Theme: red/white/blue palette. Dark navy base with red and white accents. Alternatively, white base with red+blue accents.
- Hero: full-viewport with bold flag-inspired graphic elements — diagonal stripes, star motifs, or color-block sections. Strong, direct headline.
- Texture: star or stripe motifs as subtle background patterns. Clean geometric accents. American-flag color gradients (subtle, not literal flag imagery).
- Cards: navy bg with red accent borders, white text. Or white cards with navy borders and red accent highlights. Clean, bold, no-nonsense.
- Product layout: full-width product sections with strong color blocking. Spec numbers large and prominent. "American-designed" messaging woven naturally into product sections.
- Typography: font-black, uppercase, extra-wide tracking. Direct, commanding. Think military recruitment poster, not corporate brochure.
