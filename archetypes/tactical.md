# Tactical

> Dark dominant. Precision-engineered.

## When to use

Matches brand vibes: **Bold/Edgy**, **Modern/Tech-forward**. Good fit for dealers with a military, precision-engineering, or operator-focused identity — geometric accents, corner brackets, disciplined layouts.

Currently used by: **Black Knight Drones** (accent `#EFBF04`, military-geometric, corner-bracket motif).

## Design specification

- Theme: pure black (#0a0a0a) with zinc-900 surfaces, geometric precision
- Hero: full-viewport video, geometric accent lines (horizontal rules, corner brackets), left-aligned text (not centered), "SCROLL" indicator. Use the shared `heroVideo` with `heroPoster` as fallback.
- Texture: dot-grid radial patterns, thin geometric lines extending from edges, corner bracket accents on product images and decorative elements
- Cards: gradient from-zinc-900-to-zinc-900/50 with left-side hover accent bar that animates in height, icon in bordered black square
- Product layout: circular product image display with concentric rings, corner brackets, model-tag pill badge below. Spec cards as a grid beside the circle.
- Typography: font-black (weight 900) headlines, very large (7xl), model numbers as gradient-text display elements. Eyebrow text with line-dash prefix (w-12 h-px accent line + text)
