FAQs

How many acres can I cover in a day?
Coverage varies by rate, terrain, and turnaround logistics. Many operators plan missions up to 600–800 acres* within the optimal application window depending on the aircraft and conditions.

Can it handle wet or uneven ground?
Yes. Revolution drones are designed to operate where traditional ground rigs struggle—maintaining access during narrow spray windows.

How does RTK help?
RTK provides ±4" pass-to-pass precision for consistent swath overlap and reduced input waste.

Can I switch between spraying and spreading?
Absolutely. Both platforms support interchangeable mission kits, allowing operators to move from liquid application to solids spreading quickly.
