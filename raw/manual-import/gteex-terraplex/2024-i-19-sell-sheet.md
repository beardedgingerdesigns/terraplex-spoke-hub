## Body copy

It uses RTK 4-inch precision to accurately spray 20 gallons of liquid or spread 26 gallons of solids, virtually eliminating chemical and seed waste. The I-19 can easily navigate and operate in wet, uneven, or inaccessible terrain where a ground sprayer would get stuck. This versatility allows you to switch between spraying, fertilizing, and seeding, all while drastically lowering fuel and labor costs and ensuring your operation is the most efficient and sustainable in the field.
