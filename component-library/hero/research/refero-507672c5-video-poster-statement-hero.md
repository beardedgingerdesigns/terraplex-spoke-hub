## Why this is library-worthy
The minimalist endpoint of the manifesto family: full-bleed cinematic landscape photograph, centered bold sans-serif statement overlaid in white, ONE play-button affordance in the middle of the frame, no subtext, no second CTA, no nav distraction in the band. Optionally followed by a paragraph of plain-text prose below the photograph and a SECOND identical centered-on-photo statement band, then a 3-icon credibility row. The pattern is "video-poster as hero" — the photograph IS the video poster frame, the play button is the only interactive element, and the headline is the thesis statement. Different from existing hero patterns: the [manifesto-creed hero](./refero-b86571f2-manifesto-creed-hero.md) carries a heavier sequence (declarative headline → creed photo → real-person pull quote → category cards); this pattern strips that down to JUST the centered-statement-on-photo with a play-button affordance — the lightest possible hero that still carries vision-language. Right pick when the dealer's brief is "we believe X about [farming / land / stewardship], here's a video that explains why" — vision-led without the heavy structural commitment of the full manifesto sequence.

## What to take
- Single full-bleed photograph hero band (~50–60vh, edge-to-edge, no padding rails). The photograph is a wide cinematic landscape — golden-hour farmland, distant barns, harvest field, herd in pasture, weather over crops. The horizon is roughly centered or slightly low; the photograph reads as "the place" rather than "an event."
- Centered overlay: 2-line bold sans-serif headline in white (~36–48px), short and declarative ("We're building a better food system." → for a dealer "We're rebuilding what stewardship looks like." / "We fly the equipment we sell." / "Built in the Americas, for the American Farmer."). The headline ends in a period. No subtext, no kicker label, no body paragraph in the band.
- Single circular play-button affordance (~64–80px) directly below the headline, centered. White stroked circle with a white triangle pointing right. Click expands to a full-screen video lightbox. The play button is the only interactive element in the hero — no second CTA, no link, no dropdown.
- Below the hero band, a single paragraph of plain-text prose (~14–16px, charcoal, 60–80 character line length, centered or constrained-width left-aligned) — 3–5 sentences explaining the thesis the headline asserts. This is the only prose moment between hero bands.
- Optional: a SECOND identical centered-on-photo statement band below the prose, with a different cinematic photograph (same landscape register, different scene — pasture instead of barn, harvest instead of sunrise) and a different declarative statement that builds on the first ("We're developing better standards for higher quality ingredients." → for a dealer "We're certifying every operator we sell to."). The second statement doesn't have a play button — it stands alone as type-on-photo. This stacked-band rhythm is optional; one band works as the minimal version of the pattern.
- Below the second statement band (or directly below the first if no second band), a single short paragraph of supporting prose, then a 3-icon credibility row: 3 small monochrome line-art circle-icons (~80px each), each above a 2-line bold caption + small "Learn more" link. Credibility claims are concrete ("Sustainable seafood recommended by Seafood Watch®" / "Not fed antibiotics or hormones" → for a dealer "Part 137 certified by Sam Welton" / "Built in the Americas" / "Same-day fleet diagnostics"). Icons are not feature-grid; they're a credibility footer for the vision-led header.
- Page closes with a "Join Us" centered headline + 2-line paragraph + single filled CTA pill ("Sign Up" → for a dealer "Schedule a field visit" / "Talk to a Sam Welton specialist"). One CTA, anchoring the page after the vision-led opening did the convincing.

## What NOT to take
- Don't add a second CTA inside the hero band. The pattern's restraint depends on the centered statement + play button being the ONLY chrome over the photograph. A "Learn more →" or "Watch the video" link competes with the play button and dilutes the affordance. The play button IS the "watch the video"; the conversion CTA waits until the page closer.
- Don't import this pattern without a real video to play. The play button must launch a real video — a marketing reel, a founder explanation, a documentary clip about the dealer's operation. A play button that opens a "video coming soon" modal or a generic stock loop collapses the trust register the headline built. If the dealer doesn't have a real video, drop the play button and use the [manifesto-creed hero](./refero-b86571f2-manifesto-creed-hero.md) (which carries the credibility through a real-person pull quote instead).
- Don't make the headline more than 2 lines. The pattern's affordance depends on the statement being scannable in one breath — a 3+ line headline turns the band into a paragraph and the centered-on-photo register collapses into a generic blog hero.
- Don't import Blue Apron's literal food-system copy ("better food system", "better standards", "Sustainable seafood") — that's brand-vertical-specific. The transferable move is "centered declarative statement on full-bleed cinematic photograph + single play button." For a Terraplex dealer the statements lean toward stewardship, regional pride, equipment philosophy, or operator commitment — short claims the dealer can defend.
- Don't crowd the icon credibility row with more than 3 cells. The pattern's restraint is the affordance; 4+ icons turn the row into a feature grid and the page reads as a product-block. For a real feature grid use the [editorial cream-bands product-block](../../product-block/research/refero-ecfaaa4f-editorial-cream-bands.md) below.
- Don't add scroll-triggered animation, parallax, or autoplay video on the hero band. The photograph is static; the play button is the only motion-on-click element. Parallax on the headline shifts the register from "we believe this" to "we built a fancy landing page."
- The dark-dominant register of FORGE / TACTICAL conflicts with this pattern's golden-hour landscape and white-overlaid sans-serif — both archetypes' near-black backdrop and precision-geometry rules collapse the warm-photographic-with-statement register. For dark archetypes use the [topographic layered-bg hero](./refero-ae84b62a-topographic-layered-bg.md) or the [hardware-lineup dark hero](./refero-0945a36c-hardware-lineup-dark-hero.md) instead.

## Adaptation notes (cross-archetype)
- FIELD: native fit. Photograph is golden-hour American farmland, fenceline shot, harvest scene, weather over crops — warm-lit, real, defensible (the dealer's actual region, not stock). White overlaid headline, white play button. Below the hero, the prose paragraph is in warm-charcoal at constrained width (~640px). The 3-icon credibility row uses hand-drawn line-art icons in warm-amber stroke. The "Join Us" closer CTA is an amber pill with cream label. Pairs naturally with the [editorial cream-bands product-block](../../product-block/research/refero-ecfaaa4f-editorial-cream-bands.md) for a coherent FIELD page rhythm. See [FIELD archetype](../../../archetypes/field.md).
- PATRIOT: photograph is late-afternoon American farmland — barns, weathered equipment, herds at pasture, harvest crews at sunset. The single play-button stays white. Headline in white declarative font, ending in a period. Below the hero, the prose paragraph is in deep-charcoal. The 3-icon credibility row uses deep-navy line-art icons; one icon out of three carries a barn-red accent stroke (the only saturated moment in the closer band). The "Join Us" closer CTA is a deep-navy pill with cream label. Don't overlay a flag or stripes on the photograph — the stewardship register IS the patriotic move at this scale; chrome flags would compete with the headline. Pairs with the [manifesto-creed hero](./refero-b86571f2-manifesto-creed-hero.md) on a sibling page (this hero for the lighter video-led version; manifesto-creed for the heavier sequence-led version). See [PATRIOT archetype](../../../archetypes/patriot.md).
- TERRAIN: the hero photograph reads as the page's opening "light band" — the closing icon row drops to a slightly tonal-shifted cream / clay band as the first transition into the alternating rhythm. The play button gets a thin clay or sage rim. Below the hero, add a thin topographic line motif at micro-scale beneath the prose paragraph — signals the contour-line rhythm to come. See [TERRAIN archetype](../../../archetypes/terrain.md). Pairs with the [topographic layered-bg hero](./refero-ae84b62a-topographic-layered-bg.md) on a sibling dark-band page.
- CLEAN: photograph stays cinematic but cooler — a clean equipment-in-context shot with daylight (not golden hour), or a wide-angle hangar / facility / field shot in even light. Headline in clean modern-sans (no heritage tilt), play button in white-stroked-circle with a thin brand-accent rim. Prose paragraph in brand-charcoal. The 3-icon credibility row uses brand-accent line-art icons. The "Join Us" closer CTA is a brand-navy pill. See [CLEAN archetype](../../../archetypes/clean.md). Pairs with the [centered-screenshot-trio hero](./refero-f619e700-centered-screenshot-trio.md) on a sibling page (this for the vision-led version; trio for the product-led version).
- FORGE / TACTICAL: skip — the cream-base prose paragraphs and golden-hour landscape conflict with both archetypes' dark-dominant rules. For dark-archetype vision-led openings, use the [topographic layered-bg hero](./refero-ae84b62a-topographic-layered-bg.md) (abstract dark backdrop) or the [hardware-lineup dark hero](./refero-0945a36c-hardware-lineup-dark-hero.md) (cinematic studio sweep) — different affordances, dark-archetype-native.

## Source brief

The library-worthy move is the structural cadence (centered-statement on full-bleed cinematic photograph + single play-button affordance + plain-text prose paragraph + optional second statement-on-photo band + 3-icon credibility row + "Join Us" closer CTA), reusable for a Terraplex dealer whose brief is vision-led but doesn't warrant the full [manifesto-creed sequence](./refero-b86571f2-manifesto-creed-hero.md) — a stewardship landing, an "about our region" page, an operator-commitment page, a [Sam Welton compliance philosophy page](../../../content/compliance-program.md).
