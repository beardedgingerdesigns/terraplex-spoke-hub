## Program overview

Terraplex runs an in-house, full-service compliance team that handles the FAA-side work of getting a drone operator legally in the air. The team operates as a **behind-the-scenes partner** to dealers and end customers — not a separate billing line, not an upsell. Customers across the United States have worked with Terraplex's compliance team for ag-drone regulatory issues regardless of which Terraplex dealer they bought from.

**Services covered:**
- Individual Part 107 Remote Pilot certifications (study, prep, exam coordination)
- 44807 exemption applications
- Part 137 exemptions and operating certificates
- Writing new regulations / exemption requests for FAA submission on customer behalf
- State-level compliance monitoring across the dealer network's footprint

**The pitch line on the parent site:** "Compliance Made Easy" / "360 service."
