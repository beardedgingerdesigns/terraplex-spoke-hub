# Feature Gaps

> If a dealer requests an item from **Platform capability gaps**, log it as a Follow-up in the dealer's NOTES.md — these are things the platform may build someday. If a dealer requests an item from **Terraplex editorial restrictions**, explain it's a brand-level decision, not a platform limit.

## Platform capability gaps

Things the platform can't do yet, regardless of which hub. These are future platform features. For now, handle requests with placeholder CTAs that link to `#contact` or to the dealer's social media profiles.

- **Scheduling / call-booking integration** — some dealers want customers to suggest a time for a call. Not a calendar integration. Future feature.
- **Google Reviews live feed** — pulling reviews from Google's API. Future feature.
- **Instagram grid embed** — showing latest posts. Future feature.
- **Parts ordering / inventory** — ordering from a catalog. Future feature.
- **Dealer backend portal** — dealer login to manage their own site. Future feature.

## Terraplex editorial restrictions

Things Terraplex has decided not to offer on dealer sites. These are brand-level decisions, not platform limits.

- **Blog / news section** — Terraplex decision. Content updates happen through the platform chat, not a dealer-maintained blog.
