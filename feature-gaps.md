# Feature Gaps

> If a dealer requests an item from **Platform capability gaps**, log it as a Follow-up in the dealer's NOTES.md — these are things the platform may build someday. If a dealer requests an item from **Terraplex editorial restrictions**, explain it's a brand-level decision, not a platform limit.

## Platform capability gaps

Things the platform can't do yet, regardless of which hub. These are future platform features. For now, handle requests with placeholder CTAs that link to `#contact` or to the dealer's social media profiles.

- **Scheduling / call-booking integration** — some dealers want customers to suggest a time for a call. Not a calendar integration. Future feature.
- **Google Reviews live feed** — pulling reviews from Google's API. Future feature.
- **Instagram grid embed** — showing latest posts. Future feature.
- **Parts ordering / inventory** — ordering from a catalog. Future feature.
- **Dealer backend portal** — dealer login to manage their own site. Future feature. *(See expanded scope in next two items.)*
- **Dealer portal: AI Marketing Materials Automation** — opt-in program where the platform auto-generates dealer marketing materials (spec sheets with dealer logo, digital banner ads, flyers, decals, print-ready trade-show signage) by combining the dealer's brand context (already on their site), Terraplex brand assets, and Revolution Drones / GTEEX assets. Optionally extends to a per-dealer monthly marketing budget that the system spends on auto-placed ads (digital billboards in the dealer's territory specifically discussed). Surfaced 2026-05-01 by New Heights — see [synthesis/new-heights-meeting-2026-05-01.md](synthesis/new-heights-meeting-2026-05-01.md). High-priority future feature; Justin committed to ~1 week of design ideation before the next New Heights touch-base.
- **Trade Show Support Packages** — opt-in templated kit for dealers attending farm shows. Includes booth-layout templates (10×10, 10×20, 20×20), best-practice setup guides ("spec sheet for setting up booths"), and print-ready files at correct sizes for the trade-show signage the layout needs. Distinct from the generative AI marketing system — this is a curated set of proven booth designs dealers can opt into as a package. Pilot use case: New Heights's Four State Farm Show (Pittsburgh KS, July 9–11, 2026). Surfaced 2026-05-01 — see [synthesis/new-heights-meeting-2026-05-01.md](synthesis/new-heights-meeting-2026-05-01.md).

## Terraplex editorial restrictions

Things Terraplex has decided not to offer on dealer sites. These are brand-level decisions, not platform limits.

- **Blog / news section** — Terraplex decision. Content updates happen through the platform chat, not a dealer-maintained blog.
