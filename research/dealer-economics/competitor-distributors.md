## Summary

Three US-based companies operate in adjacent space to Terraplex and its dealer network: **Agri Spray Drones** (Boonville, MO — multi-brand distributor with national dealer network), **Bestway Ag** (KY — DJI-only certified distributor), and **Rantizo** (Houston, TX — drone-application services rather than primarily a sales business). All three differ from the Terraplex/Revolution model in either product mix (multi-brand vs single-brand exclusive), in compliance offering (none provide an in-house Part 137 program comparable to [Terraplex's compliance program](../../content/compliance-program.md)), or in business model (operator services vs equipment sales).

## Why this matters for Terraplex dealers

When a customer is comparison-shopping, they're often pricing Terraplex against one of these three rather than against a foreign manufacturer directly. Knowing each competitor's actual product mix, geographic footprint, and service offering lets a Terraplex dealer position the comparison on terms that emphasize the parent brand's structural advantages: single-brand focus on Revolution Drones, in-house compliance via Sam Welton, and the farmer-owned reseller framing (see [content/positioning.md](../../content/positioning.md)). Generic "we have the best drone" framing won't land — concrete differentiation requires knowing what the alternative looks like.

## Key facts

### Agri Spray Drones (agrispraydrones.com)

- **HQ**: 13620 Old Highway 40, Boonville, MO 65233. Phone: 573-519-5000 [source: https://agrispraydrones.com].
- **Tagline**: "EMPOWERING RURAL AMERICA™" (registered trademark) [source: https://agrispraydrones.com].
- **Self-description**: "North America's most experienced distributor of agricultural spray drone technology" [source: https://agrispraydrones.com].
- **Product mix (multi-brand)**:
  - **EAVision lineup** — flagship is the J150 ("The Revolution Is Here"), J70 (mid-tier flagship-performance/right-sized), J100 (legacy), J100 Upgrade Kit
  - **DJI Agras** — T50, T40, T25, T30, T20P, T20, T10 (full T-series available)
  - **Autel** — EVO MAX 4N, MAX 4T XE, EVO Lite 6K, Alpha L35T (enterprise tier)
  - **DJI Enterprise / Delivery** — Mavic 3 series, Matrice 30T / 350 RTK / 3TD, FlyCart 30, DJI Dock 2
- **Roles offered**: drone sales, parts, custom application, dealer network ("Become A Dealer"), digital solutions (Drone Operations Center, Pix4D Fields, DigiFarm RTK subscriptions), regulations resource center, knowledge hub, eLearning courses (J100 eLearning, Part 107 Training), service portal, ASD GPT (custom AI assistant)
- **Model**: a *true distributor* — they don't make drones, they sell across all major OEMs. This is the closest structural analog to Terraplex, but multi-brand instead of single-brand exclusive.

### Bestway Ag / Bestway Drones (bestwaydrones.com)

- **Phone**: (270) 887-8550. Email: drones@bestwayag.com [source: https://www.bestwaydrones.com].
- **Self-description**: "Bestway Ag is a certified DJI Agras agricultural drone dealer/distributor. Featuring the DJI Agras T100, DJI Agras T50, DJI Agras T25 and the DJI Mavic 3M multispectral. Nation's largest brick-and-mortar dealer network" [source: https://www.bestwaydrones.com].
- **Product mix (DJI-exclusive)**: DJI Agras T100, T50, T25, plus DJI Mavic 3M Multispectral. Plus their own drone trailers (DT-Mini, DT1, DT2). Plus DJI batteries / chargers / generators / parts.
- **Pricing (publicly listed at time of capture, "Inventory Reduction Event")** [source: https://www.bestwaydrones.com]:

  - DT2 Drone Trailer (fully plumbed): **$37,500**
  - D6000 Generator: $1,475
  - T50 Spreader: $950
  - T40 Spreader: $825
  - T50 Nozzle Kit: $650
  - T40 Cooling Rack: $300

- **Notable**: explicit drone-trailer product line is a category Terraplex doesn't currently emphasize. **3rd Class Medical Waivers** for veterans with PTSD via partner "Veteran Drone Services" — a niche-but-meaningful service line some Terraplex dealers may want to consider mentioning when a veteran customer comes through.
- **Financing**: offered via Approve Payments (6-month term offerings) [source: https://www.bestwaydrones.com].
- **Risk**: as a DJI-only distributor, Bestway Ag is fully exposed to the FCC's December 22, 2025 covered-list ruling — see [research/manufacturers/dji-agras.md](../manufacturers/dji-agras.md) for context. Their inventory-reduction sale may reflect this exposure.

### Rantizo (rantizo.com)

- **HQ**: 211 Highland Cross Dr, Houston, TX 77073. Email: contact@rantizo.com [source: https://www.rantizo.com].
- **Self-description**: "Precision Agriculture and Drone Services" [source: https://www.rantizo.com].
- **Tagline**: "Spraying solutions that can save growers up to 70% on their annual chemical costs" [source: https://www.rantizo.com].
- **Model**: primarily a *services* company (custom application / drone spraying as a service) rather than a sales business. The website is thin on product specifics; emphasis is on testimonials, leadership bios, and service tiers.
- **Notable**: Houston-based — geographically closer to row-crop South Plains than the Iowa-centered Terraplex network. Possible head-to-head competition for Terraplex's Oklahoma Aerial affiliate and any future Texas expansion.
- **Footprint**: their site emphasized "follow us" social rather than location-specific operations, suggesting a smaller / more centralized operation than the Terraplex 49-location network.

## Implications

- **For dealer positioning copy**: Terraplex dealers should lead with single-brand depth (every dealer knows Revolution inside-out) rather than multi-brand breadth (Agri Spray's framing). When a customer says "I'm also looking at Agri Spray," the Terraplex counter is: "We sell the platform we use; they sell whatever's in stock. Different model, different support depth."
- **For pricing conversations**: Bestway Ag's publicly-listed prices (especially DT1 at $32K, DT2 at $37.5K) are useful comparison anchors when discussing Terraplex's trailer offerings. Don't quote Terraplex's prices in writing — but do reference Bestway as the public benchmark dealers should price against.
- **For onboarding scripts**: a Terraplex new dealer asking "what do my customers compare us to?" should know all three. Default rebuttals: vs Agri Spray = single-brand focus + farmer-owned reseller framing; vs Bestway = US-built (Revolution) vs DJI's FCC exposure; vs Rantizo = sales+service depth vs services-only.
- **For equipment recommendations**: Bestway Ag's drone-trailer product line (DT-Mini / DT1 / DT2) is a category gap in Terraplex's current portfolio. If a customer needs a trailer-with-drone package, this is a competitive vulnerability worth flagging upstream to Terraplex Ag if it persists.

## Open questions / things to revisit

- **Agri Spray Drones**'s eLearning courses and "ASD GPT" custom AI assistant suggest a meaningful tech investment. Worth tracking whether they expand this into a true tech moat.
- **Bestway Ag**'s DJI-only exposure given the FCC ruling — will they pivot to multi-brand, exit, or weather it? Affects competitive landscape over the next 12 months.
- **Rantizo**'s real revenue mix (services vs. anything else) — site is too thin to tell. May warrant a follow-up digest if a more detailed source surfaces.
